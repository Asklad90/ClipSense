ClipSense is an AI-powered video repurposing and highlight-detection workspace for streamers, podcasters, and editors. It scans long-form videos to surface high-engagement moments, auto-generates accurate captions/subtitles formatted for vertical video, and scores each clip's viral potential (High/Medium/Low) with suggested titles and hashtags. Audio extraction runs client-side for fast, lightweight processing, and an interactive waveform timeline lets you fine-tune clips before export.
